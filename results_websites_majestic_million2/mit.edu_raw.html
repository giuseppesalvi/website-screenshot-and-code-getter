Spotlight: Mar 17, 2023

Flavio Emilio Vila Skrzypek left his native country of Peru to pursue a master’s in city planning at MIT. He has made it his mission to improve land-use policy back home. “The state has accepted ‘slums’ as inevitable, but I want Peru to learn from these circumstances,” he says.

RESEARCH NEWS

DUSP researchers have built TILE2NET, an open-source tool that uses aerial imagery and image recognition to create complete maps of sidewalks and crosswalks. The tool can help planners anywhere who want to expand pedestrian infrastructure.
